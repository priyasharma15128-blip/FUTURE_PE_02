On camera — casual, slightly exasperated

Close-up on skin, then back to camera

Solution 8 – 18 sec
Hold up product, show texture on fingers
"Then I found GlowRx. It's a 15% Vitamin C serum — stable formula, no stinging, smells clean, absorbs super fast. I've been using it every morning for three weeks now, just two drops."

Natural light, no filter — show skin directly

Look directly at camera — warm, not pushy

[Text overlay: "Free sample kit · Link in bio"]
